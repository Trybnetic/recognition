# Replication of Hilbig, Erdfelder & Pohl (2010)

This repository contains information, data and materials from a replication of
Hilbig et al. (2010) experiment on a model of the recognition heuristic. The
experiment was performed for the lecture "Modeling cognitive process" held by
[Florian Wickelmaier](https://homepages.uni-tuebingen.de//florian.wickelmaier/)
at the [University of TÃ¼bingen](http://www.uni-tuebingen.de/).  
The experiment was performed by Dominik Frommeyer, Benedikt Iberl, Katharina
Naumann, Lennart Scheider & Marc Weitz.

## References
Hilbig, B. E., Erdfelder, E., & Pohl, R. F. (2010). One-reason decision making
unveiled: A measurement model of the recognition heuristic. _Journal of
Experimental Psychology: Learning, Memory, and Cognition, 36_(1), 123.
